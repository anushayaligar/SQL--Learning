
# Customers Above Average Spending Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers whose total spending is higher than the average customer spending.

## Objective

Find high-value customers who contribute more revenue than the average customer.

## SQL Concepts Used

- Subquery
- Derived Table
- GROUP BY
- HAVING
- AVG()
- SUM()

## Tables Used

- customers
- orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total spending for each customer.
2. Calculated the average customer spending across all customers.
3. Identified customers spending above the average.
4. Ranked high-value customers for business targeting.

## Findings

1. A subset of customers contributes significantly more revenue than average.
2. High-spending customers represent valuable business assets.
3. These customers are ideal candidates for loyalty and retention programs.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP customer programs.
2. Offer personalized promotions to high-value customers.
3. Monitor spending behavior to improve retention.
