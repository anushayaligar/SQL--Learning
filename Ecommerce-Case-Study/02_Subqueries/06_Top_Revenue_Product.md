
# Top Revenue Product Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the product(s) generating the highest revenue.

## Objective

Determine the most valuable products based on revenue contribution.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* MAX()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue for each product.
2. Identified the maximum product revenue.
3. Returned product(s) matching the maximum revenue.
4. Highlighted the most valuable products.

## Findings

1. A small number of products generate the highest revenue.
2. Revenue is concentrated among top-performing products.
3. High-revenue products are critical to overall business performance.

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize inventory for top-revenue products.
2. Increase marketing investment for high-performing products.
3. Analyze successful product characteristics and replicate them.
