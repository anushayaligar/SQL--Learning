# Top Revenue Customer Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the customer(s) who generated the highest total revenue for the business.

## Objective

Find the most valuable customer(s) based on revenue contribution and support customer retention strategies.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* MAX()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.
2. Identified the maximum customer revenue using a subquery.
3. Returned customer(s) whose revenue matched the maximum value.
4. Highlighted the highest-value customers.

## Findings

1. The top customer generated the highest revenue among all customers.
2. A small number of customers contribute a significant share of business revenue.
3. High-value customers are critical for long-term business growth.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP programs for top customers.
2. Offer personalized promotions and rewards.
3. Monitor high-value customers to improve retention and reduce churn.
