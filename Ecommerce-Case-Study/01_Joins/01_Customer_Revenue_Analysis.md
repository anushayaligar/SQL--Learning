# Customer Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

The Sales Director wants to identify the customers generating the highest revenue for the company. Understanding top customers helps the business improve retention, loyalty programs, and targeted marketing campaigns.

## Objective

Calculate the total revenue generated by each customer and rank customers based on their contribution to overall sales.

## SQL Concepts Used

* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* SUM()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Join the customers and orders tables using customer_id.
2. Calculate total revenue generated by each customer.
3. Group records by customer.
4. Sort customers by revenue in descending order.

## Findings

1. Anusha is the highest revenue-generating customer.
2. Total revenue generated by Anusha is ₹20,000.
3. Customers with multiple purchases contribute significantly more revenue than one-time buyers.

## Business Recommendations

1. Launch a loyalty rewards program for high-value customers.
2. Provide personalized offers to increase repeat purchases.
3. Monitor top customers regularly to improve customer retention.
