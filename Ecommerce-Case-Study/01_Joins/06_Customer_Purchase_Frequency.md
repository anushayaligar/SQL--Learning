# Customer Purchase Frequency Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers who place the highest number of orders.

## Objective

Analyze customer ordering behavior and identify loyal customers who frequently purchase from the business.

## SQL Concepts Used

* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* COUNT()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Joined customers and orders tables using customer_id.
2. Counted the total number of orders placed by each customer.
3. Grouped results by customer.
4. Ranked customers based on order frequency.

## Findings

1. Some customers place significantly more orders than others.
2. Repeat customers contribute to consistent business revenue.
3. High-frequency customers represent valuable long-term customers.

## Business Recommendations

1. Introduce customer loyalty programs.
2. Offer exclusive rewards to repeat customers.
3. Analyze purchasing patterns of loyal customers to improve retention.
