
# Payment Method Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify which payment methods generate the highest revenue and are used most frequently.

## Objective

Analyze customer payment preferences and revenue contribution by payment method.

## SQL Concepts Used

- INNER JOIN
- GROUP BY
- COUNT()
- SUM()
- ORDER BY

## Tables Used

- payments
- orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Joined payments and orders tables.
2. Calculated total orders for each payment method.
3. Calculated total revenue generated by each payment method.
4. Ranked payment methods by revenue contribution.

## Findings

1. Different payment methods contribute different levels of revenue.
2. Some payment methods are preferred by customers.
3. Revenue concentration varies across payment methods.

## Business Recommendations

1. Promote high-performing payment methods.
2. Offer incentives for preferred payment channels.
3. Improve payment experience for frequently used methods.
